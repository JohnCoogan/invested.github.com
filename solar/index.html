Total Cost per Watt

(current US$)

This value represents the average total cost of an additional installed watt of solar capacity. It includes the cost of the PV module, inverter, balance of systems and installation. Our cost per Watt estimates were based on data from Green World Investor, Eco Friendly Mag and the U.S. Department of Energy.
